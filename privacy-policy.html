Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 February 2026

Who we are

Pely.ai is an AI workflow tools consultancy founded by Joseph Rigby, based in Manchester, UK. We are in the process of incorporating as a limited company and will update this policy with our Companies House registration number once available.

What data we collect

When you use our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your message

We do not collect any other personal data through this website.

Why we collect it

We collect this data solely to respond to your enquiry. We will not use your information for marketing, sell it to third parties, or use it for any purpose other than replying to you.

How your data is processed

Our contact form is powered by FormSubmit, a form submission service. When you submit the form, your data is forwarded directly to info@pely.ai. FormSubmit does not store your submission data permanently — it acts as a relay to deliver your message to us via email.

Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-focused analytics tool. Plausible does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and does not track you across websites. All data is aggregated and anonymous. No individual visitor can be identified.

Cookies

This website does not set any cookies. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any other kind of cookie.

Data retention

We retain your contact form submissions only for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry and for reasonable record-keeping. We do not maintain a mailing list or CRM from contact form submissions.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Request that we correct any inaccurate data
  • Request that we delete your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)
